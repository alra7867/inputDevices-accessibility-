Learning from Magnifier

I recognize how hard it would be for the people with low vision to work on the computer screen. Especilly how they navigate throught the web pages would have been really difficult. I used twitter after navigating my page and it was a complete mess for me. More then half of my page was hidden and I was really frustrated on not knowing on how to get out of it. After completing this assignment, I became even more motivated to do something for the people with disabilities of all type.
