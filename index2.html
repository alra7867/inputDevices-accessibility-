Trends in education

Below mentioned are some of the educational sub-fields that require alot to be done!

  • Accessibility problems related to education
  • Bringing computational thinking using IOT and Natural user interfaces
  • Blended MOOCS
  • Scaffolding techniques in education
  • Incorporating gamification in the classroom settings
  • Exer-games(Games that involve people in some kind of exercise rather then sitting on just screens)
  • Empowering womens in the STEM
  • Connected learning envirnments for underrepresented communities
  • Using equity to bring change in poor and rural setting schools

These are some of the challenges that are currently we face in this field. Feel free to contact me , if you have some kind of cool idea, we can start working on it! I am super thankful to you for reading this stuff!
